Hi I'm Ruben,

I’m an art director based in Amsterdam. I studied at the Design Academy Eindhoven and graduated in 2007 with the GooglePoetry Project. After my study I worked for several ad agencies. From there I experienced the love of making tastefully campaigns. From commercials to activations to handmade woodprints.

I’m interested in design, art direction, digital, UX, graphics, film, storytelling blablabla; I just love to communicate, especially in a ditigal way. That why I'm learning code right now!

Take a ride through my portfolio or let's meet up for a coffee!

Cheers!
